Garden Adventure Story

Ollie and his best furry friends Chester and Jonty, love visiting Aunty Kate. She has a big garden to explore. There are all sorts of treasures to discover. On the first trip down to the bottom of the garden, Chester dug up an old bone. Could it be from a dinosaur? On the second trip down, Jonty discovered a huge feather. Could it be from an eagle? On his third trip down, he uncovered a buried shell. Could the sea have been up here? On his final trip down he discovered an old piece of pottery. Could it be from the Viking times? It was time to go. They had discovered so much!
